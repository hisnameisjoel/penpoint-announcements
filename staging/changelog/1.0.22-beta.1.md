::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Spellcheck** *(Beta)* — Spell-as-you-go underlines with right-click suggestions. Your characters, locations, and items are automatically whitelisted so invented names are never flagged. Manage your personal dictionary in Settings → Spellcheck.
- **Discord Rich Presence** — Optionally show your writing activity in Discord status. Enable in Settings → Community & Updates. Your writing content is never shared.
- **Collapsible Sections Everywhere** — The collapsible section headers from Character Sheets are now on Items, Locations, and Chapters too. Click any header to tuck it away — preferences are saved per page.
- **Set Numbered/Unnumbered at Creation** — Uncheck "Numbered chapter" right in the creation dialog. The chapter number preview updates live.

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Whole-Manuscript Scanner** — Scan all chapters at once from a right-click on your book title. Finds characters, locations, and items, then links everything in one pass.
- **Find & Replace Upgrades** — Both fields always visible, last search remembered, swap button, continue-across-manuscript offer, and Enter in the Replace field searches and replaces in one step.
- **Items System** — Items can now be assigned to Locations, and you can add notes on how a character connects to an item. Icon picker expanded, item type cap raised from 10 to 50.
- **Refreshed Book Templates** — All genre templates now ask the right questions. New Romance template added. RPG renamed to D&D 5e.
- **Customizable Paragraph Spacing** — Choose Tight, Normal, or Relaxed in Settings → Interface & Manuscript.
- **Notebook Polish** — Image zoom presets menu, double-click-to-fit, correct item counts in smart folders, and matching folder layout in list mode.
