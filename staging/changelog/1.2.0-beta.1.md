::party-popper:: **Penpoint v1.2.0 Beta 1 — New & Improved Chapters!**

This is a major feature release that reimagines how you build and write your books through the reworking of the Chapters page. Now you have more control as to how you can build, plan, and write your prose!

::feather:: **New Features**

- **All-new Chapters Page** - Chapters now mirror what you find in Manuscript Mode. See and add details to both chapters and scenes, and customize how your chapter page layout looks just like you can with Characters. Go to the Settings -> Chapter Layout Setup to customize how your chapter page looks.

- **Build Your Own World's Calendar** - Your book now has its own calendar system. Pick Standard (Gregorian) for real-world stories, Day Counter for stories told by day number ("Day 47"), or go wild with Custom and design a fantasy calendar from scratch. Your own months, weekdays, festival periods, leap days, and BCE/CE-style epochs. Every scene date uses your chosen mode automatically. Calendar content can be seen via the Calendar Block in the Chapters Page, and this work paves the way for the future Timeline Page refactor!

- **Link Chapters to Character Arc Plot Points** - You can now assign chapters and scenes to individual plot points on your character arcs, just like story arcs. Open any plot point to link chapters, or view them as a read-only list. Linked chapters also show in the compact list view on the arc block.

- **Customize Your Book Covers** - Pick from 12 preset colors, mix your own with the color picker, layer on a texture like Grain or Leather, or upload your own cover image. Hide Penpoint's title overlay if your image already has it. Your choices travel with your book through backups.

- **New "Alice" Theme** - A soft cherry blossom pink theme for those who like their writing space a little more lovely. Find it in Settings, Interface.

- **Version Protection for Your Books** - If you open a book on a newer Penpoint version, then try to open it on an older version, you'll get a clear warning asking you to update first. Your book stays safe.

- **Search Works in Manuscript Mode** - The Glorious Searchbar (Ctrl+K) now works while you're writing. Search for a character, location, item, chapter, or anything else. Click a result to open it in your Minipen sidebar without leaving the manuscript. Scene results jump straight to that scene in the editor.

::sparkles:: **Improvements**

- Every word counts: the community word count now shows every word ever written in Penpoint in more detail.
- Filter Items by Location: select locations in the sidebar to show only matching items
- Find Characters by Nickname: search by alias (e.g. "Lizzy") instead of full name
- Smoother Startup: update prompts and announcements queue sensibly instead of piling up
- Better Backup Restore: access history from three places, choose whether to overwrite or create a new copy
- Total Word Count in Footer: see "1,234 words (50,000 total)" at a glance
- Right-Click in Outline: quickly add a new chapter from empty space
- Keyboard Navigation: arrow keys move visually, Alt+Left/Right navigates back and forward cleanly
- Faster Search After Editing: results update the moment you open the searchbar

- Fixed "Word Count Stats After Midnight" showing the wrong daily count
- Fixed cut-and-paste reducing word count unexpectedly
- Fixed Notebook panel widths drifting when switching between pages
- Fixed typing cursor hiding behind the formatting toolbar
- Fixed search result highlights becoming hard to read on hover
- Fixed location renames not updating the sidebar list until app reload
- Fixed Back button not clearing item selection on Items page
