::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Chapter Drafts** — Scene Drafts now work for single-scene chapters too! Right-click any chapter in the outline to save a snapshot, view old versions, or restore a previous draft. If you later split the chapter into scenes, your saved drafts carry over automatically.

- **Find & Replace with Formatting** — The Find & Replace bar now has a collapsible "Formatting" section. Use **Match** to search only bold, italic, or underlined text. Use **Apply** to add formatting to replacements — like swapping a nickname for an italicized foreign name in one go. Works with Replace All and "Replace in rest of manuscript" too.

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

- **Find & Replace stays in sync when switching chapters** — Results no longer show stale matches from the previous chapter after switching.
- **Smart quotes after punctuation** — Quote marks after periods and other punctuation now produce curly quotes correctly.
- **Curly quotes after em dashes** — Quote marks ending interrupted dialogue now face the right direction.
- **Minipen Notes sorting** — Numbered folders sort naturally (1, 2, 10 instead of 1, 10, 2), sort changes apply immediately, and the menu matches the rest of the app.
