::party-popper:: **Penpoint v1.2.0 Beta 2 — More Fixes and Polish!**

Beta 2 brings additional fixes and polish on top of the major features in Beta 1. If you haven't tested Beta 1 yet, everything below is new to you. If you have, scroll to the bottom for what changed since then.

::feather:: **New Features**

- **Build Your Own World's Calendar** - Your book now has its own calendar system. Pick Standard (Gregorian) for real-world stories, Day Counter for stories told by day number ("Day 47"), or go wild with Custom and design a fantasy calendar from scratch. Your own months, weekdays, festival periods, leap days, and BCE/CE-style epochs. Every scene date uses your chosen mode automatically.

- **All-new Chapters Page** - Chapters now mirror what you find in Manuscript Mode. See and add details to both chapters and scenes, and customize your chapter page layout just like you can with Characters. Go to Settings, Chapter Layout Setup to get started.

- **Link Chapters to Character Arc Plot Points** - You can now assign chapters and scenes to individual plot points on your character arcs, just like story arcs. Open any plot point to link chapters, or view them as a read-only list.

- **Customize Your Book Covers** - Pick from 12 preset colors, mix your own with the color picker, layer on a texture like Grain or Leather, or upload your own cover image. Hide Penpoint's title overlay if your image already has it. Your choices travel with your book through backups.

- **New "Alice" Theme** - A soft cherry blossom pink theme. Find it in Settings, Interface.

- **Search Works in Manuscript Mode** - The Glorious Searchbar (Ctrl+K) now works while you're writing. Search for a character, location, item, chapter, or anything else. Click a result to open it in your Minipen sidebar without leaving the manuscript. Scene results jump straight to that scene.

- **See Where Your Locations Appear** - Every location now shows which chapters it appears in, right on its detail page. Previously, chapter-location connections were only visible from the Chapters side. In edit mode, you can also assign or remove chapter connections directly from the location.

- **Version Protection for Your Books** - If you open a book on a newer Penpoint version and then try to open it on an older version, you'll get a clear warning asking you to update first.

::sparkles:: **Improvements**

- Filter Items by Location: select locations in the sidebar to show only matching items
- Find Characters by Nickname: search by alias (e.g. "Lizzy") instead of full name
- Smoother Startup: update prompts and announcements queue sensibly instead of piling up
- Total Word Count in Footer: see "1,234 words (50,000 total)" at a glance
- Community word count now shows every word ever written in Penpoint in more detail
- Right-Click in Outline: quickly add a new chapter from empty space
- Keyboard Navigation: arrow keys move visually, Alt+Left/Right navigates back and forward cleanly
- Faster Search After Editing: results update the moment you open the searchbar
