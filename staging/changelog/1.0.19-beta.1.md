# What's New in v1.0.19-beta.1

## ::party-popper:: New Features

- **Find & Replace in Manuscripts** — Hit Ctrl+F to search your manuscript, or Ctrl+H for Find & Replace. Every match is highlighted and you can navigate between them with Enter/Shift+Enter. Replace one at a time or all at once. Works at any scope — scene, chapter, or full book.

- **Manuscripts in the Searchbar** — The searchbar now searches your prose too. Find a line of dialogue, a description, or a character's inner monologue from anywhere in the app. Click a result and Penpoint takes you straight to the scene, scrolls to it, and highlights the match.

## ::feather:: Improvements

- **@ Mention Selection Persistence** — Arrow key or mouse selections in the @ mention dropdown now stick as you refine your search, instead of resetting every keystroke.

- **Plain Text Import** — Import .txt files (including Google Docs exports) into your manuscript. Paragraph breaks, chapter headings, and horizontal rules are detected automatically.

- **Manuscript Toolbar Adapts to Your Window** — The formatting toolbar intelligently collapses into a "More" menu as your window narrows, starting with the least-used tools.

- **Smarter Content Scanner** — Common words like "castle" and "shadow" are no longer flagged as character names when used as regular prose. Named locations like "Shadow Keep" are still caught.

- **Deleted Blocks Dialog** — Now shows the correct icon and name for every block type and explains that your underlying data is safe.

## ::bug:: Bug Fixes

- **Copy-Paste from External Editors** — Text from Ellipsus, VSCodium, and dark-themed editors no longer arrives highlighted in yellow. Fixed LibreOffice Writer pasting the wrong font.

- **Word Count Tracking Accuracy** — The Activity Tracker no longer counts your entire manuscript as "new words" every time you leave manuscript mode.

- **DOCX Import Fixes** — Blank lines preserved, Heading 1/2 styles recognized (including non-English Word), soft line breaks handled correctly.

- **@ Mentions in Arcs** — Using @ mentions in Arc rich text fields no longer shows "..." instead of the actual name.

- **Flowstate Timer Overlapping Toolbar** — On smaller screens, the timer now drops into its own panel below the toolbar.

- **Manuscript Word Count** — Fixed slight inaccuracy with longer texts.

- **Ctrl+Home / Ctrl+End** — Now correctly scrolls to the top or bottom of your manuscript.

- **Blank Book Template Selector** — The template dropdown no longer appears empty.

- **Text Field Cursor Jumping** — Typing in plain-text blocks no longer causes cursor jumping or lost words.

- **Character Edit Mode Sticking** — Returning to the Characters page no longer forces Edit mode.

- **Content Scanner Fixes** — Fixed single-quote dialogue false positives, scrolling issues, alias capitalization, and several other detection edge cases.

- **Activity Tracker Timing** — Writing time no longer keeps ticking when you switch to another app.

- **Manuscript Outliner Fixes** — New chapters stay in their folder, drag handles are always visible, and dragging is smoother.
