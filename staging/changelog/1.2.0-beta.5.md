This is the fifth beta of v1.2.0, a pre-release candidate with continued fixes and polish before the full production release.

::party-popper:: **New Features**

**Build Your Own World's Calendar** - Your book now has its own calendar system. Choose Standard (Gregorian) for real-world stories, Day Counter for stories told by day number, or design a fully custom fantasy calendar from scratch: your own months, weekdays, festival periods, leap days, even BCE/CE-style epochs. Every scene and chapter date uses your chosen mode automatically.

**Customize Your Book Covers** - Pick from 12 preset colors or mix your own with a color picker. Add a texture like Grain, Linen, Leather, or Parchment. Upload your own cover image, cropped to fit. Hide Penpoint's text overlay if your image already has the title. All choices travel with your book through backups and restores.

**Link Chapters to Arc Plot Points** - Assign chapters and scenes to individual plot points on your character arcs, just like you already could with story arcs. Open any plot point in edit mode to link chapters, or switch to view mode to see them as a read-only list.

**Customize Your Character Arc View** - The Character Arc page now has a gear icon in the visualizer header to control what's visible. Choose to show plot points for all characters, just the active one, or none at all. Toggle "Always show labels" off for a clean comparison view; you can still hover or click any keyframe to reveal its label. The active character's line is also a bit thicker now so it stands out from any overlays.

**Search Works in Manuscript Mode** - The searchbar (Ctrl+K) now works while you're writing. Search for a character, location, item, chapter, or anything else. Clicking a result opens it in your Minipen sidebar. Scene results jump you straight to that scene.

**See Where Your Locations Appear** - Every location now shows which chapters it appears in, right on its detail page. Assign or remove chapter connections directly from the location in edit mode.

**New "Alice" Theme** - A soft cherry blossom pink theme. Find it in Settings, Interface, alongside the other themes.

**Version Protection for Your Books** - If you open a book on one computer with a newer version of Penpoint, then try to open it on an older version, Penpoint will explain what's happening and ask you to update first.

**Reset Demo Book** - The Sherlock Holmes demo book now has a Reset option in its right-click menu. Restore it to its original state any time.

::feather:: **Improvements**

**Tag Viewer for Multi-Scene Chapters** - The Tag Viewer now works properly across chapters with multiple scenes, showing tags at the right level so you can see what's tagged where without confusion.

**Refreshed Chapters Tutorial** - The Chapters tutorial has been rewritten to walk you through the new block-based layout system, with clearer steps and better example data. Crosslinks between chapters and other elements are also smoother throughout the tour.

**Filter Items by Location** - Filter your Items list by location. Select one or more locations in the filter sidebar and only items tied to those locations will show.

**Find Characters by Their Nickname** - Search by a character's alias anywhere you add a character to something. Type "Lizzy" and Elizabeth Bennet shows up.

**Smoother Startup Experience** - Updates, data upgrades, and announcements now queue up and appear one at a time in a sensible order. The analytics prompt also has a new "Not now" option.

**Every Word Counts** - The community word count in your stats panel now shows every word written in Penpoint since launch, right down to the last one.

**Keyboard Navigation in Notebook and Items** - Arrow keys now move to the visually adjacent card instead of jumping alphabetically, and Alt+Left/Alt+Right cleanly goes back or forward without also shifting your highlight. The Items page also picked up Up and Down arrow keys for jumping between rows.

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

**Character Arc Overlays Lining Up** - When comparing multiple character arcs, plot point markers on background character arcs could drift out of alignment with the active character's, even when both arcs had the same number of points. They line up properly now.

**Arc Scene Assignment Clarity** - Assigned scenes in your arc plot points now show a filled square so you can see at a glance what's linked. The Assign and Remove buttons are always visible, no hovering required.

**Chapter Description Data Preserved Across Migrations** - Fixed a v1.1.2 to v1.2.0 upgrade path where chapter descriptions could be lost during the migration to the new block-based layout. Every upgrade path now preserves your chapter descriptions completely.

**Lazy Scene Creation Race Condition** - Fixed a race condition where rapid clicks during scene creation could occasionally create duplicate or orphaned scenes. Scene creation is now reliable even under heavy clicking.

**Crosslink Display Fixes** - Characters now show the correct scene numbers in their chapter list (instead of array positions). Items and Locations now show scene-level drill-down, not just chapters. Story Arc plot points now filter to assigned scenes only in view mode.

**Right-Click Submenus Staying on Screen** - Fixed submenus getting cut off at screen edges, especially near corners. When a submenu flips sides to fit, it now sits flush against the parent menu.

**Cleaner Scene Names** - Adding a second scene to a chapter no longer creates two rows labeled "Empty Scene." New scenes are named "Scene 1," "Scene 2," and so on.

**Domain URLs in Manuscripts** - Fixed a rare bug where domain endings like .it could sneak into your manuscript as hidden links. Your text is now safe from sneaky URLs.

**Cutting Text No Longer Reduces Your Word Count** - Using cut and paste to move text around no longer affects your daily word count.

**Notebook Panel Widths Drifting** - If you resized the folder tree or preview panel in the Notebook and then switched to another page and came back, the panels would shift slightly each time. Fixed.

**Manuscript Cursor Hiding Behind Toolbar** - The typing cursor could scroll up behind the formatting toolbar, letting you type text you couldn't see. Fixed.

**Search Result Highlights on Hover** - When hovering over a search result, the matched search term could become hard to read. Fixed.

**Back Button Not Clearing Item Selection** - On the Items page, pressing Back right after selecting an item didn't actually deselect it, which threw off the back and forward history. Fixed.

**Calendar and Chapter Card Polish** - Several calendar UI improvements and chapter card display fixes, plus better submenu positioning throughout.

**Notebook Rename Fixes** - Fixed a rename box that would flash and disappear before you could type. Fixed a renamed note title not updating in the open viewer.

**Export Title Page Remembers Your Book** - The Title field in the export dialog now always shows your current book's name, not whatever you last exported.

**Manuscript Folders Staying Put** - Empty folders in the Manuscript outliner no longer swap positions when you come back after creating several in a row.

**Location Renames Now Update the List** - Renaming a location in edit mode now updates the name in the left-hand list immediately, without needing to reload.

**Faster Search Results After Editing** - Fixed an issue where old text could still appear in search results for several seconds after you corrected it in the manuscript.

**Word Count Stats After Midnight** - Fixed the "Manuscript size (+X today)" stat showing the wrong number when writing past midnight.

**Closing the App During the Welcome Tour** - The X button now properly closes Penpoint during the first-run welcome tour.

Thank you for testing Penpoint staging! Report any issues before production release.
