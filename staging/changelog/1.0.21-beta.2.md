::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Notebook Revamp** — The Notebook has been completely rebuilt into a full reference board. Store images alongside your notes with a built-in lightbox viewer for zooming and panning. Add Shortcuts to jump straight to any character, location, or entity. Organize everything into nested folders up to five levels deep with a collapsible folder tree. Richer note card previews now show proper line breaks, list formatting, and @mentions.

- **Draggable Relationship Map** — Drag characters around on the Relationship Map to arrange them however makes sense for your story. Your custom positions are saved per book and remembered between sessions. Right-click to unpin, or hit "Reset Positions" to start fresh.

- **Folder Word Counts** — Hover over any folder in the Manuscript Outline to see the total word count for everything inside it.

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Smarter Content Scanner** — The scanner now predicts whether each discovery is a Character, Location, or Item based on grammatical context, so you spend less time sorting through results.

- **Simpler Backup Settings** — Set how often to back up and how many days to keep — that's it. Recent backups are kept in full, older ones are thinned automatically.

- **Full Export Preview** — The export preview now renders all your scenes with accurate page counts and proper scene breaks from the moment it opens.

- **Smarter Help Guide** — The Help Guide now knows what page you're on and links you directly to the most relevant article.

- **Performance on Slower Machines** — Reduced unnecessary background work across the app for smoother typing and less visual stutter.

- **Spellcheck Toggle in Manuscript Settings** — Turn spellcheck on and off directly from the Manuscript settings panel.

- **Richer Community Stats** — Now tracks worldbuilding word counts, book count, export counts by format, and general time of day (same privacy rules as before).

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

- **Phantom Backups When Idle** — Automatic backups no longer run when you haven't actually changed anything.

- **Traits Not Syncing** — Creating, editing, or deleting traits in Settings now takes effect immediately on all character sheets.

- **Relationship Character Picker** — Clicking back to fix a relationship pick now shows your full character list.

- **Text Disappearing When Clicking Buttons** — Typing into a rich text field and quickly clicking a button no longer causes text to vanish.

- **Page Crashes on macOS** — Fixed a crash when navigating between pages, especially on Apple Silicon Macs.

- **More Accurate Writing Stats** — Session time properly pauses when you alt-tab or step away. Pasting no longer inflates your word count.

- **Back/Forward Navigation** — Both directions now work correctly, including in the Notebook and when returning from the User Guide.

- **Window Recovery on Disconnected Monitors** — Penpoint now detects off-screen windows and moves them back to your main display.

- **Manuscript Folder Reordering** — Dragging folders to reorder now works even with just two folders.

- **@Mention Fixes** — Deleted arcs/plot points no longer appear in results. Copy-pasting @mentions preserves links. Enter after Escape creates a new line.

- **Paragraph Breaks After @Mentions** — Editor upgrades no longer merge paragraphs around @mentions.

- **Content Scanner + Nicknames** — Merging in the scanner no longer replaces nicknames, highlighting works, and ignoring names no longer blocks related words.

- **Mini Penpoint Fixes** — Long notes no longer push Pin/Archive buttons into the text. Location profile pictures now show in the sidebar.

- **Searchbar Ghost Results** — Deleted entities no longer linger. Clicking a plot point navigates correctly.

- **Manuscript Export Preview** — Changing options no longer resets to page 1.

Thank you for testing Penpoint staging! Report any issues before production release.
