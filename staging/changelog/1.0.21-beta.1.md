::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Notebook Revamp** — The Notebook has been rebuilt into a full reference board. Store images with a built-in lightbox viewer, add Shortcuts to jump to any entity, organize into nested folders up to five levels deep, and enjoy richer note card previews with proper formatting.
- **Draggable Relationship Map** — Drag characters around to arrange your relationship map however you like. Your custom positions are saved per book and remembered between sessions. Right-click to unpin, or hit "Reset Positions" to start fresh.
- **Folder Word Counts** — Hover over any folder in the Manuscript Outline to see the total word count for everything inside it.

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Export Preview Shows Your Full Manuscript** — The export preview now renders all your scenes with accurate page counts and proper scene breaks from the moment it opens.
- **Performance on Slower Machines** — Reduced unnecessary background work across the app. Typing should feel smoother, especially on older hardware.
- **Spellcheck Toggle in Manuscript Settings** — Turn spellcheck on and off directly from the Manuscript settings panel.
- **Richer Community Stats** — Now tracks worldbuilding word counts, book count, export counts by format, and general time of day (same privacy rules as before).

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

- **Relationship Character Picker** — Clicking back to fix a relationship pick now shows your full character list instead of just the one already selected.
- **Text Disappearing When Clicking Buttons** — Typing into a rich text field and quickly clicking a button no longer causes text to vanish.
- **Page Crashes on macOS** — Fixed a crash when navigating between pages on Apple Silicon Macs.
- **More Accurate Writing Stats** — Session time properly pauses when you alt-tab or step away. Pasting text no longer inflates your daily word count.
- **Back/Forward Navigation Fixes** — Back and forward arrows now work correctly everywhere, including the Notebook and User Guide.
- **Window Recovery on Disconnected Monitors** — Penpoint now detects when it's off-screen and moves itself back to your main display.
- **Manuscript Folder Reordering** — Dragging folders to reorder now works correctly even with just two folders.
- **@Mention Fixes** — Deleted arcs/plot points no longer appear in @mention results. Copy/pasting @mentions preserves links. Enter after Escape works properly.
- **Paragraph Breaks After @Mentions** — Paragraph breaks around @mentions are now preserved correctly during editor migration.
- **Content Scanner + Nicknames** — Merging a word no longer replaces nicknames, highlighting works, and ignoring names no longer blocks related words.
- **Mini Penpoint Notes Scroll Bug** — Pin and Archive buttons no longer appear in the middle of long notes.
- **Mini Penpoint Location Images** — Location profile pictures now show in the sidebar.
- **Searchbar Ghost Results** — Deleted entities no longer linger in search results.
- **Manuscript Export Preview** — Changing formatting options no longer resets the preview back to page 1.
