# What's New in v1.0.19

::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Find & Replace in Manuscripts** -- Hit Ctrl+F to search your manuscript, or Ctrl+H to open Find & Replace. Every match is highlighted and you're scrolled right to the current one. Navigate with arrow buttons or Enter/Shift+Enter. Replace one at a time or all at once. Works at whatever scope you're viewing — a single scene, a whole chapter, or the full book. Select text before pressing Ctrl+F and it auto-fills your search.

- **Your Manuscripts in the Searchbar** -- The Glorious Searchbar now searches your prose too. Find a line of dialogue, a description, a character's inner monologue — if you wrote it, the searchbar finds it. Click a manuscript result and Penpoint takes you straight to that scene, scrolls to the matching text, and highlights it. Works from anywhere in the app.

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Plain Text Import** -- You can now import .txt files into your manuscript — including exports from Google Docs. Penpoint detects paragraph breaks, chapter headings, and horizontal rules automatically.
- **@ Mention Selection Persistence** -- If you highlight a result in the @ mention dropdown, that selection now sticks as you refine your search — no more losing your place every keystroke.
- **Manuscript Toolbar Adapts to Your Window** -- The formatting toolbar now adjusts as your window gets narrower. Instead of buttons piling up, they tuck into a "More" menu one group at a time.
- **Smarter Content Scanner** -- The scanner no longer flags common words like "castle," "shadow," or "village" as character names. If you have a location called "Shadow Keep," it'll still catch "Shadow Keep" — but not every shadow.
- **Deleted Blocks Dialog** -- The Deleted Blocks panel now shows the correct icon and name for every block type, and explains that your data is safe if a block is permanently removed.

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

- Fixed copy-paste from dark-themed editors (Ellipsus, VSCodium) arriving highlighted in yellow, and pasting from LibreOffice bringing in wrong fonts
- Fixed manuscript word count being slightly off with longer texts
- Fixed Ctrl+Home / Ctrl+End not scrolling to the top or bottom of your manuscript
- Fixed DOCX import stripping blank lines, missing Heading 1/2 styles, and mishandling soft line breaks
- Fixed Activity Tracker counting your entire manuscript as "new words" every time you left manuscript mode
- Fixed @ mentions in Arc rich text fields showing "..." instead of the actual name
- Fixed Flowstate timer overlapping the formatting toolbar on smaller screens
- Fixed blank book template selector appearing completely empty
- Fixed text field cursor jumping around and undoing words while typing on character sheets
- Fixed character edit mode sticking after creating a character and navigating away
- Fixed Content Scanner flagging common words like "With" and "As" when using single-quote dialogue
- Fixed various Content Scanner issues: scrolling, alias capitalization, names merging across dialogue, italic/bold confusion, and multi-word name detection
- Fixed Activity Tracker "time writing" still ticking when switching to another app
- Fixed Manuscript Outliner: new chapters staying in folders, folder drag handles hidden until hover, drag flickering, and dragging chapters out of folders
