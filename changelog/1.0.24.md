::party-popper:: **New Features in v1.0.23**

- **Import a Manuscript When Creating a Book** — When creating a new book, choose "Import a Manuscript" instead of starting from scratch. Pick your file, preview and customize chapters, optionally scan the whole manuscript for characters, locations, and items, then name your book and go. Everything lands in your new book ready to work with.
- **Half-Width Blocks for Every Block Type** — Place any block side-by-side on your character sheet — not just Traits and Numbers. Tags next to a Character Arc, Appearance paragraph next to Age. Click the split-column icon in a block's toolbar to toggle it half-width.
- **Spellcheck in Worldbuilding** *(Beta)* — Spellcheck now works in your character, location, and item notes — not just in Manuscript Mode. Right-click an underlined word for suggestions or to add it to your dictionary.
- **Character Page Right-Click Menus** — Right-click anywhere on the Characters page for quick actions. Right-click a character in the list to add, delete, or change sort/view options. Right-click the detail panel to toggle edit mode, change importance, swap profile images, and more.
- **Manuscript Right-Click Menu** — Undo, Redo, Select All, and Paste without Formatting are now in the right-click context menu.
- **Smarter Help Guide Search** — The search bar now searches the full content of every article, not just titles. Results show the article name, section, and a highlighted snippet.
- **Revamped Tutorials** — Every interactive tutorial has been overhauled. Tooltips point exactly where they should, example data is more realistic, and the Notebook page gets its own full walkthrough.
- **Expanded New User Walkthrough** — New slides, smoother animations, and better guidance for first-time users.
- **Manuscript Import & Export in Settings** — Import and export your manuscript prose directly from the Import & Export settings page, reorganized into Worldbuilding and Manuscript sections.
- **Em Dash Shortcut** — Three dashes (`---`) now make an em dash automatically.
