::party-popper:: **New in v1.0.26**

- **Autocorrect Settings** — New controls under Settings → Spellcheck & Autocorrect let you choose your dash behavior (Standard: `--` → em dash, or Typographer: `--` → en dash, `---` → em dash) and toggle smart curly quotes on or off.

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Chapter Titles Are Now Optional** — Leave the title blank when creating a chapter and it shows up as "Chapter 1", "Chapter 2", etc. Add a title whenever you're ready — or never.
- **Copy Text from the Compendium and Help Guide** — You can now highlight and copy text in the Compendium and Help Guide. Navigation links are also a distinct color so they're easier to spot.

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

- **Strikethrough in Manuscript** — Strikethrough was missing from the toolbar and its keyboard shortcut didn't work. Both are now available alongside Bold, Italic, Underline, and Highlight.
- **Chapter Outline Changes Appearing Deleted** — Editing a chapter outline and switching sections could make your recent changes appear to vanish. Your work was saved the whole time — fixed.
- **Book Scanner Accuracy** — The scanner was occasionally linking entities to the wrong chapters and showing duplicate aliases. Matching is now more precise.
- **Tags Now Work in Quick Create** — The Tags block in Quick Create (Ctrl+N) now shows your tags as selectable options.
- **Timeline Not Scrolling** — If you had enough characters to fill the Timeline vertically, the rest were cut off. The Timeline now scrolls.
- **Scene Drafts Preserved After Versioning** — Fixed scene drafts going missing after creating a manuscript version.
- **Spellcheck False Positives on Quoted Words** — Words in single quotes were getting flagged even when spelled correctly. Fixed.
- **Novel View No Longer Indents Centered Text** — The "Novel" paragraph setting no longer indents centered or right-aligned text.
- **Discord Status Stuck on "Worldbuilding Mode"** — Your status now reflects where you actually are from the moment you open the app.
- **Manuscript Tutorial Freezing at Step 3** — Fixed on high-resolution or custom-scaled displays.
- Additional fixes for Tags section card previews, spellcheck underlines in view mode, character list scrolling, Notebook back button, tutorial polish, Items tutorial restart, toolbar button spacing, and Notebook right-click menu positioning.

Thank you for using Penpoint!
