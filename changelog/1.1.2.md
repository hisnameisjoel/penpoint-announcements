# Penpoint v1.1.2

::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Chapter Drafts** — Scene Drafts now work for chapters that haven't been split into scenes. Right-click any single-scene chapter and you'll find "Chapter Drafts" — save a snapshot, view old versions, or restore a previous draft. If you later split the chapter into scenes, your saved drafts carry over automatically.
- **Find & Replace with Formatting** — A new collapsible "Advanced" section in Find & Replace lets you search by formatting (bold, italic, underlined) and apply formatting to replacements. Perfect for tracking down foreign words or swapping a nickname for an italicized name in one operation. Works with Replace All and "Replace in rest of manuscript" too.
- **Whole Word Matching** — A new toggle in Find & Replace matches whole words only — so searching for "Yes" won't catch "Yesterday." On by default. Penpoint remembers all your Find & Replace settings between sessions.
- **Find & Replace Toolbar Button** — A magnifying glass button in the manuscript toolbar opens and closes Find & Replace. It stays highlighted while the panel is open.

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

- **Notebook Display** — Fixed the note editor overflowing off-screen when the sidebar was pinned, and note titles squishing at high display scaling.
- **Flowstate No Longer Forces Fullscreen** — Flowstate and fullscreen mode are now independent — use either, both, or neither.
- **Find & Replace Chapter Sync** — Switching chapters with Find & Replace open now re-searches the new content and clears previous replace counts.
- **Smart Quotes After Punctuation** — Typing a quote after a period no longer produces a straight quote.
- **Curly Quotes After Em Dashes** — Quote marks after an em dash now face the correct direction.
- **Minipen Notes Sorting** — Numbered folders sort naturally (1, 2, 10), sort changes apply immediately, and the menu matches the rest of the app.

Thank you for using Penpoint!
