# Penpoint v1.1.1

::party-popper:: **Penpoint's First Purchase**

The About page now recognizes **Jendriel** as Penpoint's Founding Supporter — the very first person to buy a license. Thank you!

::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Zen Mode for Manuscripts** — Press F11 or click "Fullscreen Mode" in the footer to fade away everything but your words over a smooth transition. Hover the edges to slide out the chapter outline, Minipen, or formatting toolbar on demand. New Settings under **Fullscreen Mode** let it activate automatically when you start typing and exit after idle — Flowstate triggers it too.
- **The Worldbuilding Toolbar Guide** — A new help article walks you through every icon and button in the toolbar. Find it in the User Guide under "The Worldbuilding Toolbar."

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Backup Restore Upgrade** — Backup history is now accessible from three places: Book Selector, Settings, and the toolbar. Choose **Overwrite Current Book** or **Restore as New Book**, with an automatic safety backup either way.
- **Total Word Count in the Footer** — See your current scope alongside your total manuscript word count at a glance. Click to toggle the total on or off.
- **Right-Click Anywhere in Your Outline** — Right-click empty space in the Manuscript outline to quickly add a new chapter or group.
- **Notebook Drag-and-Drop** — Drag folders between levels and into new positions with cleaner visual feedback.
- **Faster Note Creation** — New notes drop your cursor right into the title. Press Tab to jump to the writing area.
- **User Guide on Smaller Screens** — The Help Guide is now much more readable on smaller screens and narrower windows.

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

- **Dragging Notes to the Binder** — No longer deletes them.
- **Undo in the Notebook** — Ctrl+Z no longer replaces your note with content from the previous one.
- **Item Title Overwriting** — Fast-clicking between items no longer writes the wrong title.
- **Character-Item Relationship Notes** — Notes no longer vanish when switching to view mode.
- **Editing Trait Options** — Renaming a trait option now updates all characters automatically.
- **macOS Sequoia Typing** — Disabled system autocorrect that caused cursor jumps and duplicate text.
- **@Mention Fixes** — Escape closes suggestions, custom item types show real names, Home key works correctly, Tab confirms suggestions.
- **Items After Tag Filtering** — New items are now editable immediately after filtering by tags.
- **Minipen Search** — Clicking a result now jumps to and highlights your match.
- **Daily Word Count** — Deleted words now subtract from your count. Net-negative days reflect honest editing sessions.
- **Unnumbered Chapter @Mentions** — Prologues and interludes no longer show wrong chapter numbers.
- **Writing Stats at Midnight** — Daily delta no longer disappears when writing past midnight.
- **Writing Streak** — Fixed incorrect streak counts after opening the app.
- **Character List Scroll** — Returns instantly to your last position instead of scrolling through the list.
- **Item Type Dropdown** — Updates immediately when changed in edit mode.
- **Empty Folder Drag** — Empty folders now stay where you drop them.
- **Smart Quotes & Backspace** — No longer requires two presses to delete a curly quote.
- **Relationships at Half-Width** — Fixed large gaps in side-by-side layout.
- **Writing Stats Panel** — No longer gets cut off at screen edges.
- **Notebook Paste Overflow** — Pasted text now wraps properly.

Thank you for using Penpoint!
