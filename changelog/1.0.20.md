# What's New in v1.0.20

::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Quick Create (Ctrl+N)** -- Press Ctrl+N (or Cmd+N on Mac) from anywhere in Penpoint to instantly create a new Character, Item, Location, Chapter, Note, or Tag without leaving the page you're on. Type to filter the list, pick your entity type, fill in the essentials, and you're done. Three ways to finish: **Create** keeps you where you were, **Create & Open** takes you to the new entity, and **Create + Mention** drops an @mention link into whatever text field you were writing in. In Manuscript Mode, "Create & Open" seamlessly opens the entity in the Mini Penpoint sidebar so you never lose your place.

- **Inline Images** -- You can now insert pictures directly into any rich text editor -- character bios, location descriptions, chapter notes, arc details, notebook entries, and your manuscript. Click the image button in the toolbar, pick a file, and it drops right in with a decorative frame. Right-click any image for "Save to Disk" or "Delete Image." Images are stored inside your book folder, so they travel with your backups automatically.

- **Character Nicknames & Aliases** -- Give each character a nickname or alias. Set it in the Alias field on the character's edit screen, and it shows up everywhere -- on cards, in chapters, on the relationship map, in the timeline, and across the compendium. Search by alias using the searchbar or @mentions, and the Content Scanner recognizes aliases when scanning your manuscript.

- **Collapsible Character Blocks** -- Every block on your character sheet can now be collapsed by clicking its header. A small chevron appears to the left of each block title. Your collapse preferences are remembered separately for the Characters page and the Minipen sidebar.

- **Spellcheck Enabled** -- Penpoint now has built-in OS-level spellcheck in the manuscript editor and rich text fields. Misspelled words get the familiar red underline, and right-click gives you suggestions. ProWritingAid and Grammarly desktop apps work with Penpoint too -- no setup needed.

- **Content Scanner Now Detects Tags** -- The Content Scanner can now match your existing Tags alongside Characters, Locations, and Items. Tag matches show up with their color so you can spot them at a glance.

- **Quick Add in Content Scanner** -- Manually add entities directly from the scanner results. Hit "Quick Add" to create a new row, pick the type, type in the name, and it'll be created and linked to the chapter when you apply. You can also use the chain icon to link it as an alias to an existing entity.

- **Create New from Any Assignment Dropdown** -- When assigning a relationship, location, item, or chapter to an entity, you can now create a brand new one right from the dropdown. Start typing a name that doesn't exist yet and a "Create New..." option appears.

- **Cloud Storage Warning** -- If your book storage location is inside a cloud-synced folder (OneDrive, Dropbox, Google Drive, or iCloud), Penpoint now shows a warning explaining why that can cause problems and points you toward Secondary Backups.

- **Right-Click Menus on Book Library** -- Right-click any book in the Book Library to quickly access Edit, Duplicate, Export, Backup, Restore, or Delete. Right-click on empty space to create a new book or access library options.

- **Right-Click Menu in Text Editors** -- Right-click inside any rich text field to get Cut, Copy, Paste, Insert @Mention, and Insert Image. Paste preserves your formatting.

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Manuscript Export Wizard Polish** -- The export wizard is easier to navigate: scene break style names are now in plain English, the preview panel is larger so you can actually read it, chapter rows are fully clickable instead of just the tiny checkbox, and the step counter adapts to your export path.
- **Mini Penpoint Character Names** -- Character names in the Mini Penpoint sidebar were oversized, especially at larger font sizes with a profile picture set. They're now properly scaled to fit the sidebar.
- **Spellcheck Toggle** -- Turn off spellcheck underlines in Settings → Interface → Spellcheck. Handy if you write in a language your system doesn't support or use lots of invented names.
- **Smarter Automatic Backups** -- Automatic backups now only run when you've actually made changes. No more identical backups rotating out older ones while you're away.
- **Transparent Images Stay Transparent** -- Profile pictures and inline images with transparency (like character art or logos saved as PNG) now keep their transparent backgrounds.
- **Export Now Includes All Pictures** -- The "Include Pictures" toggle now exports profile pictures for characters, locations, and items, plus any images embedded in your writing.
- **Better Backup Defaults** -- Backups now run every 20 minutes (was 30), keep 12 hourly snapshots (was 8), and retain 10 days of daily backups (was 30).
- **Add Character Button Always Visible** -- The "Add Character" button now lives above the character list instead of inside it.
- **Editor Shortcuts** -- Indent/Outdent shortcuts now show Tab and Shift+Tab, which work on every keyboard layout.
- **Announcement Link Button** -- Announcements with a link now show a small copy-link button next to the action button.
