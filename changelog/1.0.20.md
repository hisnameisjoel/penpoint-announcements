# What's New in v1.0.20

::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Quick Create (Ctrl+N)** -- Press Ctrl+N (or Cmd+N on Mac) from anywhere to instantly create a new Character, Item, Location, Chapter, Note, or Tag without leaving the page. Three ways to finish: **Create** keeps you where you were, **Create & Open** takes you to the new entity, and **Create + Mention** drops an @mention into whatever you were writing. In Manuscript Mode, "Create & Open" opens the entity in the Mini Penpoint sidebar so you never lose your place.

- **Inline Images** -- Insert pictures directly into any rich text editor -- character bios, location descriptions, chapter notes, arc details, notebook entries, and your manuscript. Click the image button in the toolbar, pick a file, and it drops in with a decorative frame. Right-click any image for "Save to Disk" or "Delete Image." Images live inside your book folder and travel with your backups automatically.

- **Character Nicknames & Aliases** -- Give each character a nickname or alias that shows up everywhere -- on cards, in chapters, on the relationship map, in the timeline, and across the compendium. Search by alias using the searchbar or @mentions, and the Content Scanner recognizes aliases when scanning your manuscript.

- **Spellcheck Enabled** -- Built-in OS-level spellcheck in the manuscript editor and rich text fields. Misspelled words get the familiar red underline, and right-click gives you suggestions. ProWritingAid and Grammarly desktop apps work too -- no setup needed.

- **Collapsible Character Blocks** -- Every block on your character sheet can now be collapsed by clicking its header. Your collapse preferences are remembered separately for the Characters page and the Minipen sidebar, so you can keep different views for different workflows.

- **Create New from Any Assignment Dropdown** -- When assigning a relationship, location, item, or chapter, you can now create a brand new one right from the dropdown. Start typing a name that doesn't exist yet and a "Create New..." option appears -- one step instead of two.

- **Right-Click Menus** -- Right-click any book in the Book Library for quick access to Edit, Duplicate, Export, Backup, Restore, or Delete. Right-click inside any rich text field for Cut, Copy, Paste, Insert @Mention, and Insert Image.

- **Quick Add in Content Scanner** -- Manually add entities directly from scanner results. Hit "Quick Add," pick the type, type the name, and it'll be created and linked to the chapter when you apply. You can also link it as an alias to an existing entity.

- **Content Scanner Now Detects Tags** -- The scanner now matches your Tags alongside Characters, Locations, and Items. Tag matches show up with their color so you can spot them at a glance.

- **Cloud Storage Warning** -- If your book is stored inside a cloud-synced folder (OneDrive, Dropbox, Google Drive, or iCloud), Penpoint now warns you and points you toward the safer alternative: Secondary Backups.

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Smarter Automatic Backups** -- Backups now only run when you've actually made changes. No more identical backups rotating out older ones while you're away.
- **Better Backup Defaults** -- Backups now run every 20 minutes (was 30), keep 12 hourly snapshots (was 8), and retain 10 days of daily backups (was 30). These kick in automatically when you update.
- **Export Now Includes All Pictures** -- The "Include Pictures" toggle now exports profile pictures for characters, locations, and items, plus any images you've embedded in your writing.
- **Transparent Images Stay Transparent** -- Profile pictures and inline images with transparency (like character art saved as PNG) now keep their transparent backgrounds intact.
- **Manuscript Export Wizard Polish** -- Scene break style names are now in plain English, the preview panel is larger, chapter rows are fully clickable, and the step counter adapts to your export path.
- **Spellcheck Toggle** -- Turn off spellcheck underlines in Settings → Interface → Spellcheck. Handy if you write in a language your system doesn't support or use lots of invented names.
- **Add Character Button Always Visible** -- The "Add Character" button now lives above the character list instead of inside it -- no more scrolling to the top.
- **Mini Penpoint Character Names** -- Character names in the sidebar were oversized at larger font sizes. Now properly scaled to fit.
- **Editor Shortcuts** -- Indent/Outdent shortcuts now show Tab and Shift+Tab, which work on every keyboard layout.
- **Announcement Link Button** -- Announcements with a link now show a copy-link button next to the action button.

- Fixed community stats showing negative word counts after deleting text
