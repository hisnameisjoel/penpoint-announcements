# What's New in v1.0.21

::party-popper:: **New Features**

- **Notebook Revamp** — The Notebook has been completely rebuilt into a full reference board. Store images alongside your notes with a built-in lightbox viewer. Add Shortcuts to jump straight to any character, location, or entity. Organize everything into nested folders up to five levels deep with a collapsible folder tree. Richer note card previews now show proper line breaks, list formatting, and @mentions.

- **Draggable Relationship Map** — Drag characters around on the Relationship Map to arrange them however makes sense for your story. Your custom positions are saved per book and remembered between sessions. Right-click to unpin, or hit "Reset Positions" to start fresh.

- **Writing Timer** — A count-up stopwatch in the header bar for tracking your writing sessions. Works in both Worldbuilding and Manuscript modes — start, pause, and reset from the clock dropdown. Auto-pauses during Flowstate and resumes when it ends. Optionally set it to auto-start when you open a book.

- **Clock Widget** — See the current time right in the header bar. Toggle between 12-hour and 24-hour formats, show or hide seconds, or turn it off entirely.

- **Quick Mode Switch (Ctrl+M)** — Press Ctrl+M (Cmd+M on Mac) to instantly flip between Manuscript and Worldbuilding mode from anywhere.

- **Outline Word Counts** — Hover over any folder, chapter, or scene in the Manuscript Outline to see its word count at a glance.

::feather:: **Improvements**

- **Flowstate Focus Mode** — Flowstate now collapses both the outline sidebar and the Minipen when you start a session for a cleaner writing canvas. Both panels restore when you stop.

- **Full Export Preview** — The export preview now renders all your scenes with accurate page counts and proper scene breaks from the moment it opens.

- **Smarter Content Scanner** — The scanner now predicts whether each discovery is a Character, Location, or Item based on grammatical context, so you spend less time sorting through results.

- **Simpler Backup Settings** — Set how often to back up and how many days to keep — that's it. Recent backups stay in full, older ones are thinned automatically.

- **Smarter Help Guide** — The Help Guide now knows what page you're on and links you directly to the most relevant article.

- **Performance on Slower Machines** — Reduced unnecessary background work for smoother typing and less visual stutter.

- **Spellcheck Toggle in Manuscript Settings** — Turn spellcheck on and off directly from the Manuscript settings panel.

- **Richer Community Stats** — Now tracks worldbuilding word counts, book count, export counts by format, and general time of day (same privacy rules as before).

::bug:: **Bug Fixes**

- **Manuscript Spacing & Indentation** — Reduced paragraph gaps so your prose reads more naturally. Tab now indents the entire paragraph as a block.

- **Traits Not Syncing** — Trait changes in Settings now take effect immediately on all character sheets. Deleting a trait cleanly removes its blocks from all characters.

- **Story Arc Fixes** — Fixed label overlaps, missing anchor indicators, selection jumping, color picker mismatches, and chapter/tag assignments silently unlinking when editing plot points.

- **Text Disappearing When Clicking Buttons** — Typing into a rich text field and quickly clicking a button no longer causes text to vanish.

- **Page Crashes on macOS** — Fixed a crash when navigating between pages, especially on Apple Silicon Macs.

- **Phantom Backups When Idle** — Backups no longer run when you haven't actually changed anything.

- **More Accurate Writing Stats** — Session time properly pauses when you alt-tab or step away. Pasting no longer inflates your word count.

- **Back/Forward Navigation** — Both directions now work correctly, including in the Notebook and when returning from the User Guide.

- **@Mention Fixes** — Deleted arcs/plot points no longer appear in results. Copy-pasting @mentions preserves links. Paragraph breaks around mentions are preserved during editor upgrades.

- **Content Scanner + Nicknames** — Merging no longer replaces nicknames, highlighting works, and ignoring names no longer blocks related words.

- **Manuscript Sidebar Search** — Tags, plot points, and keyframes now appear in search results. "Open in Worldbuilding" takes you to the right entity.

- **Window Recovery** — Penpoint detects off-screen windows and moves them back to your main display.

- **Manuscript Folder Reordering** — Folders now swap on contact, even with just two folders.

- **Mini Penpoint Fixes** — Long notes no longer push buttons into text. Location profile pictures now show in the sidebar.

- **Searchbar Ghost Results** — Deleted entities no longer linger. Clicking a plot point navigates correctly.

Thank you for using Penpoint!
